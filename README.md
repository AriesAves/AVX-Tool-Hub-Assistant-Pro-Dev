AVX Tool Hub Assistant Pro

AVX Tool Hub Assistant Pro is an AI-powered printing support and knowledge management platform designed to assist Consultants, Technicians, and Supervisors in providing accurate, consistent, and efficient customer support across printing operations.

The system serves as a centralized assistant that helps users quickly access printing standards, production guidelines, product information, troubleshooting resources, and company knowledge bases. It also tracks assistance activities, saves conversations, and provides supervisor-level monitoring and reporting tools.
Key Features
For Consultants
AI-powered chat assistance for customer inquiries
Product and service information lookup
Printing standards and specification guidance
Production requirement assistance
Knowledge base search and topic location
Order number tracking
Multi-channel support options including Chat, Email, and Voice
Automatic conversation saving for future reference
For Technicians
Technical troubleshooting support
Production and prepress guidance
Printing specification verification
Product knowledge assistance
Knowledge base search capabilities
Order-related support tools
Saved conversation history for reference and documentation
For Supervisors
Team activity monitoring and reporting
Consultant and Technician performance tracking
Login and attendance monitoring
Job number assistance tracking
Searchable conversation history
Productivity and utilization reports
Historical records by employee, date, and job number
Centralized oversight of support activities
Purpose

AVX Tool Hub Assistant Pro improves operational efficiency by providing instant access to critical printing knowledge, reducing response times, standardizing support practices, and creating a searchable repository of assistance records. The platform enables teams to deliver faster, more accurate customer service while giving supervisors visibility into team performance and support activities.
